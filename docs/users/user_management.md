## Access to AWS infrastructure policies in the AIDR platform

1. User access will be reviewed **every 3 months** until further notice. If you have not used the platform in this time, your access will be removed **permanently**. This applies regardless of the level of access you have
2. All users will be required to sign a memorandum of understanding to ensure fair, safe and transparent usage
3. All users must enable MFA access on their account
